Meyra Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Information We Collect

Meyra may use location data only when users enable features such as nearby mosques, Qibla direction, or prayer times.

Assistant Feature

Meyra includes a local assistant feature that processes user-entered religious questions entirely on-device.

This feature does not use OpenAI, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any third-party AI provider.

Assistant question content is not transmitted to external AI services.
